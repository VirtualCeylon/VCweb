Virtual Ceylon presents ancient history in a modern video-game format, weaving a narrative that combines pulse-pounding action with enthralling tales of days past.
It gives the player the opportunity to step into the shoes of a modern archaeologist who clashes with his artefact-robbing nemesis, both toppling into an arcane gateway to the past.
As the archaeologist races desperately through time and space to escape his implacable enemy, it is you who must guide him to safety.
Collect artefacts, craft tools, and explore the old world as you engage in the race of your lifetime.
Available for free on Google Play Store.

News Updates


IOS Version Coming Soon!

Our team is working on a version of Virtual Ceylon that is playable on iOS devices. This will be coming to Apple App Store in 2023 and will broaden the scope of our vision top create a means of entertaining and engaging historical education that will appeal to all.
Coming March 2023!

Crafting System in the Works!

Ever feel like it’s too difficult to escape your foe? Fear not! You can soon gather materials during runs and create awesome weapons and tools in your crafting station to use on your enemy.
  • Stab your foe with stone daggers
  • Weave saddles on which to ride elephants
  • Topple rocks with levers

Coming January 2023!

Anuradhapura Era on the way!

The next phase of Virtual Ceylon: Race the Ages will be released in late 2023. Centering around the Anuradhapura era of Sri Lanka’s past, it will take you to the age of kings and heroes for a brand new adventure!
  • Exciting ancient urban chases
  • A brand-new Hunter mechanic
  • A new era to explore and enjoy

Coming in 2023!
